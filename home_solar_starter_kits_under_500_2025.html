How We Recommend Picking

  1. Capacity: 240–500Wh covers routers/lights/laptop hours. Higher Wh = longer runtime.
  2. Inverter: Pure sine preferred for electronics.
  3. Inputs: USB-C PD (at least 60–100W) simplifies laptop charging.
  4. Panel quality: Foldable 100W panels are practical; check connectors (MC4/XT60).
